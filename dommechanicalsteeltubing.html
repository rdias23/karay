DOM Mechanical Steel Tubing

KARAY METALS, INC. offers DOM Mechanical Steel Tubing under specification A513 T5 with many sizes of grades 1020 and 1026 in stock.


ASTM A513 - 08a is the standard specification for Electric-Resistance-Welded (ERW) Carbon and Alloy Steel Mechanical Tubing. Type 5 (T5) covers drawn over a mandrel (DOM). Grades 10XX are nonresulphurized Carbon Steels with Manganese 1.00% maximum.
